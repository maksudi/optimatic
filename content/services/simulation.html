--- title: Simulation ---

Simulation can help your business make better decisions by reviewing many different scenarios quickly. Why customers are not happy? Why orders are not delivered on time? Why expensive equipment is idle? These are some of the questions businesses ask themselves about their operations.

Simulation is used to model processes in businesses and obtain the result of execution without running the actual processes in the real world. By modelling different what-if scenarios sumulation can help businesses make the right decisions at the right time. Each simulation results in various metrics and metrics of different scenarios can be compared, thus making it easier to choose the better option. These metrics may include financial indicators, quality and tonnage measurements, energy usage indicators and many others.

Simulation combined together with predictive modelling and optimisation is extremely powerful combination giving your business the ability to predict and optimise your business based on data from the future.
