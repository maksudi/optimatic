Predictive Modelling and Analytics

Successful businesses need to have as much knowledge as possible about what can happen in the future. Knowing possible outcomes can help businesses to make better decisions and be prepared for possible future events.

How predictive modelling can help

If you're not confident about questions such as:

  • What raw materials you'll need to order for the next month (or year)
  • Whether (and/or when) you need to run maintenance operations on your equipment
  • What type of campaign you need to run for better target reach

...then you could greatly benefit from a predictive modelling system in your organisation.

Understand the value of your data and make better decisions with our predictive modelling skills

Our team stays up to date with the latest research and has a knowledge and experience with a wide variety of predictive modelling techniques and algorithms. We employ a combination of traditional as well as modern algorithms based on artificial intelligence. The result is a predictive modelling system that learns from your data over time and which "tweaks" and "validates" itself over time.

With the right predictive modelling solution your business can benefit through more accurate demand forecasts for your products, reduced back-orders, fewer lost sales, and reliable inventory levels.

Some of the applications our predictive modelling can be applied to include:

  • Demand forecasting (seasonal, steady, trending, etc.)
  • Replenishment of materials and finished goods
  • Pricing and discounting
  • Promotions and incentives
