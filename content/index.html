How we can help you

Adaptic Solutions is an independent data science consultancy. We help you use hard evidence to drive business decisions and improve outcomes for customers, staff and shareholders.

Adaptic can work with you on...

  • Strategy: Create a strategy for identifying and proving data science opportunities in your organisation, in consultation with all levels of your team.
  • Capability: Develop your data science capability by helping you select and acquire hardware and software, as well as developing the processes, organisational structures, and skills to realise your strategy.
  • Data engineering: Lead or support your data engineering work to integrate your systems and set up modelling and visualisation tools.
  • Data science: Lead or support your data science efforts to design learning experiments, select and train models, and evaluate their effectiveness.

Why data science?

By using advanced architectures and machine learning algorithms, data can be used from both core and peripheral systems including web-site logs, contact-centre notes and call transcripts, store monitoring and external social media streams, together creating a much richer view of your customers and stakeholders than has been possible before.

Up until now, more data has meant more integration work as well as more expensive and slow engineering. Cluster computing (either in the cloud or on-premise) together with powerful end-user tools offer a new approach that can drastically reduce the time and cost of generating insights from your data.
